How To Play Minesweeper

Objective: Dangerous mines that are randomly spread out all across the board, hiding under tiles. You must flag all of these mines, and reveal the remaining tiles on the board. As you reveal tiles, numbers will be displayed of the amount of mines neighboring each tile. Use these as clues in your hunt for mines.

Controls:

  • Right click to toggle tiles between flagged and unflagged
  • Left click to reveal tiles

Rules:

  1. You must click on a tile one at a time
  2. If you reveal a tile that is a bomb, you will die.
  3. Once you reveal all non-bomb tiles, you will win the game!
